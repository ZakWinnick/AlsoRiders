What we do

Three things, mostly.

01

Ride together.

Group rides scheduled in regions where members are. Casual paces, real coffee stops, no drop. The first event in any city is always free.

02

Trade notes.

The TM-B is new and software-defined. Ride modes, firmware updates, frame swaps, Connect+ tricks — we figure it out faster as a group.

03

Show up.

Meet other owners at coffee, at trailheads, at cross-brand EV gatherings. The bike is a vehicle. The club is the people who ride them.
