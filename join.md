## How to join

1. **Tap the link below** to open our Heylo group.
2. **Create a Heylo account** (or sign in if you already have one — most clubs use Heylo).
3. **Introduce yourself** in the welcome chat. Tell us your city, whether you own an ALSO yet, and which top frame you have or want.
4. **Pick your region's chat** so you only get pings for events near you.
5. **Show up to a ride.**

## What you get

- **A real community of ALSO owners** organized by region, not algorithm.
- **Group rides and meetups** in your metro area, plus invites to bigger annual gatherings.
- **A place to ask questions** about firmware, ride modes, frame swaps, charging, accessories, and everything else as the platform evolves.
- **Photos, route ideas, and notes** from other owners who've already taken the trip.
- **First access to anything we organize** — group buys, demo days, partner perks if and when they happen.

## FAQ

**Do I need to own an ALSO to join?**
No. Future owners, reservation holders, and the bike-curious are all welcome. Roughly half the value of the club is getting smart on whether the bike is right for you before you commit.

**Is there a paid tier?**
Not at launch. We're starting free and seeing what the community wants. If we add paid options later, it'll be optional — the core community stays free.

**Is this an official ALSO club?**
No. We're an independent, owner-led group. We're reaching out to ALSO to be friendly and ideally get their tacit blessing, but we don't speak for the company and they don't speak for us.

**I'm not in California. Does this club work for me?**
Yes. The club is national. Heylo lets us organize by region — so when there are three or more owners in your metro, you'll see meetups in your area. If you're the first ALSO owner in your city, we'll help you host the first one.

**Why Heylo and not Discord / Slack / Facebook / Meetup?**
Heylo is built for in-person clubs. It does events, RSVPs, chats organized by topic, member profiles, and waivers in one place — without the noise of a general-purpose chat app. NorCal EVs and the Bay Area Rivian Club both run on it. It's free for members.

**What if I want to host an event?**
Please do. Hop on Heylo and message an admin — we'll help you set it up, promote it across the region, and figure out anything else you need.

**How do I contact the club?**
Easiest way: send a message via the Heylo group. For anything that needs to happen outside Heylo, email <a href="mailto:{{ site.contact_email }}">{{ site.contact_email }}</a>.
